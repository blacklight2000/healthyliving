A. Staying Alive, and Liking It:

1. Do you talk trash to complete strangers?

2. Does it matter that you have the light when you are in the crosswalk of an intersection?

3. Do you look at both sides of a one-way street before you cross it?

4. You witness a crime in progress. Do you call 911, or do you physically intervene?

5. Do you check that you cell is loaded before you leave the apartment?
